International Concert Festival is a musical concert festival, with celebrity artist performance and a Music Awards Ceremony.

Main Program
Introduction and Performance

Listen to our Chairman giving a brief history of the festival and groove to various renowned artists from different countries performing live.

PlayFest

Following the success of previous years PlayFest returns for 2023. A full day of performances split into multiple sessions. PlayFest continues to go from strength to strength, giving local amateur and professional musicians a platform to perform.

Center on Tour

Transformative performances from the banks of the Hudson and across the globe. The possibility to perform live on a magnificent stage in front of an audience and music industry professionals.

Solo Performance

Live performance by chosen celebrity artists where our judges would nominate the top performers. The power of music to compel attention and to stir emotions lends an especially fascinating aura.

Award Ceremony

On this closing ceremony awards would be distributed on the closing day of the event to those deserving artists on different categories.
