Celebration of music is the celebration of life. Cheers to the power of music. The International Concert Festival, known as ICF event is an annual six-day music and concert festival with the motto ‘Music for Peace, Music for All’. Started at Kolkata in 2011 artists from around the world and different states of India participate in this celebration of global music.
